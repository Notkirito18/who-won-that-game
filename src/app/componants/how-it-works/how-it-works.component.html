WELCOME to Who Won That Game!
Who Won That Game is a quizz game about football (soccer) games from of famous compititions and teams.
1. Choose Mode
When you click PLAY you can choose the game mode by choosing the compitition you will be asked about, so if you are a big fan of UEFA Champions League show us how much you know about it, or if you love international football choose World Cup and get that best score.
2. Gameplay
During the game, you will be presented with a fixture and you have to choose who won that game, if you choose correctly, you get 1 point and go to the next round, you guess wrong and you get a strike if you get 3 strikes it's game over.
3. Providing game result
If you are confident that you know the result of the game, you can provide the game result, if you get it right you get 2 extra points, if you get the number of goals wrong but the result is still right you will get 1 point and 1 strike, if you get the number of goals wrong and also the result is wrong you will get 2 strikes. so be carefull and only use the provide game result when you are sure.
